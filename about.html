About Me: Veterinary Surgeon in East Midlands, Liverpool & Manchester

I'm Dr. Sonia Sebastiao, an RCVS registered veterinary surgeon with more than six years of focused experience in small animal and emergency surgery. I support clinics that need dependable surgical expertise, whether for planned procedures, urgent interventions, or temporary locum cover.

My training has centred on safe anaesthesia, precise soft tissue technique, and evidence-based peri-operative care. I work closely with clinical teams to keep communication clear, minimise delays in treatment decisions, and improve outcomes for both patients and owners.

My professional philosophy is simple: compassionate, technically excellent surgery delivered with strong teamwork. I prioritise patient welfare, practical treatment planning, and postoperative continuity so clinics can feel confident in every case pathway.

Clinics choose to work with me because I bring calm decision-making under pressure, flexible availability, and a collaborative style that fits seamlessly into existing workflows. I regularly complete CPD to keep skills current and aligned with modern UK veterinary standards.
